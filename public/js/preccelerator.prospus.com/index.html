The Preccelerator® Program is an accelerator for early-stage startups offered to select companies out of the Santa Monica office of Stubbs Alderton & Markiles, LLP that provides interim office space, sophisticated legal services, mentorship, curriculum, investment strategy and access to a strategic perks portfolio with the objective of helping you grow your idea from business concept to funded startup. The Preccelerator® Program provides these benefits to as many as 10 promising young startups per term in separate growth tracks.

WHAT WE OFFER

Free 24/7 co-working space on a rolling basis in sought after Santa Monica, including printer and copier access, wireless internet and access to conference rooms for meetings and presentations.

Be proactive in making sure your incorporation, contracts and equity structures are set up correctly before you get too far down the evolutionary path.  Access to real-time legal advice and transactional legal services on site under our standard $2500 startup fixed-fee arrangement.  Fixed-fee package can also be customized to fit your legal needs.  Access to non-billable non-substantive legal advice while using the co-working space. To view SA&M’s practice offerings, click here. 

Access to customized curriculum workshops each term to fit each cohort’s specific needs, plus evening content and networking events and activities. Over 50+ per year!  Each term culminates with a Demo Day to investors and the community – attended by over 150 people each term!

Access to our network of investors through investment strategy sessions, Meet the VC Luncheons, Demo Day and targeted investor introductions.

Access to a robust Preccelerator Perks Portfolio with over $450,000 worth of discounted services and benefits to help you succeed and cut overhead expenses.  To view the current perks package,
click here.

Access to a group of seasoned mentors that can assist in the growth and success of your company.  Mentors provide regular office hours and a “value-first” approach.  To view the full list of mentors, click here.

How does my company apply?

Online applications for the Preccelerator® Program are now open! To qualify for acceptance, please fill out our online application. Once submitted, someone from our review committee will contact you. Deadline for application submissions for the October Class 11 is September 1, 2018.
